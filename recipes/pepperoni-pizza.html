Pepperoni-Pizza

Description

This classic Pepperoni Pizza is the ultimate comfort food—crispy, golden crust layered with rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and perfectly seasoned pepperoni slices. Whether you're making the dough from scratch or using a store-bought base, this recipe delivers that irresistible pizzeria-style flavor right from your oven. It’s quick, customizable, and perfect for a family dinner, a weekend treat, or a fun cooking night with friends!

Ingredients

  • 1 ready-made pizza base (or homemade dough)
  • ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 1 to 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 20–30 slices of pepperoni
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for brushing the crust)

Steps

  1. Make the dough (or use store-bought): Mix yeast, warm water, sugar, flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Roll out the dough into a pizza base on a floured surface.
  3. Add toppings: Spread pizza sauce, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and layer pepperoni slices.
  4. Season with oregano if desired and brush the crust with olive oil for a golden finish.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is crispy and the cheese is bubbly. Slice and enjoy!
