Lasagna

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food—a warm, hearty dish that layers pasta, rich tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and savory meat or vegetables. Whether you’re serving it for a family dinner or a special gathering, this classic Italian dish is sure to satisfy. Easy to prepare and incredibly satisfying, lasagna brings people together around the table for a delicious and unforgettable meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (boiled)
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, 1.5 cups of mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, parsley (optional), and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce. Add a layer of noodles, followed by half of the cheese mixture. Repeat the process with another layer of sauce, noodles, and the remaining cheese mixture.
  4. Finish with a final layer of noodles, sauce, and the remaining mozzarella. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Let the lasagna sit for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy!
